Objective

Use the arrow keys (up, down, left, right) to guide the snake and eat as much food as you can! As the snake consumes food, it gets longer. The game becomes more challenging as the snake grows longer and faster, making it harder to avoid collisions. The game ends if the snake hits a wall or collides with its own body. In the home page you have the ability to change the colour theme of your snake and adjust the difficulty level.
